A Journey Through Life

            A Journey Through Life: I am Krisalyn Domingo, a 21-year-old woman hailing from the picturesque town of Lingsat in Sta. Maria, Ilocos Sur, Philippines. My life's journey has been shaped by a series of experiences and milestones that have led me to where I am today. Born on the 4th of April in 2002, in the serene town of Silag, Sta. Maria, Ilocos Sur, I was brought into this world as a member of the Roman Catholic faith. My Filipino citizenship is a source of pride, and I carry with me the rich cultural heritage and traditions of our nation. My parents, Cristio S. Peroja and Normalyn C. Domingo, have been my guiding stars. My father, is a hardworking farmer, tilling the land and providing for our family with unwavering dedication. My mother, is a loving and dedicated housekeeper, ensuring that our home is a place of comfort and warmth. In terms of my educational journey, I embarked on this path at Kinabiti Elementary School, where I laid the foundations of my academic knowledge. I continued my education at the Catholic High School of Pilar for my secondary studies, where I not only gained knowledge but also developed valuable life skills. My quest for knowledge led me to the Ilocos Sur Polytechnic State College for my tertiary education, where I have been expanding my horizons and acquiring the skills necessary for a successful future. My journey through life has been characterized by a commitment to learning, an appreciation for my cultural heritage, and a deep respect for my family. I look forward to the adventures that lie ahead, driven by the lessons I've learned and the support of those who have been with me every step of the way. With a blend of determination and humility, I am ready to embrace the opportunities that the future holds, secure in the knowledge that my roots and values will continue to guide me on this incredible journey.
